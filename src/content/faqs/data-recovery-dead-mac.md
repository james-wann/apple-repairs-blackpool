question: Can you recover data from a Mac that won't turn on?
answer: Usually yes. We assess whether the failure is at the logic board, power, or storage level. If the drive itself is intact, we can image it and recover files even if the rest of the machine is unrecoverable. For physically damaged drives (failed mechanical or controller-level failures), we work case-by-case.
